Avtyrrians

Avtyrrians are bipedal amphibians that make their home on oceanic worlds where most of their population lives in deep underwater cities. Their average height is around 1.5 meters. Due to their low-testosterone levels they do not have much muscle mass and are quite frail in nature. Do not let this fool you though. Avtyrrians are the only species to have a 100% active psychic population, and their minds can be dangerous opponents when necessary.

Panasians

The open plains and thick bioluminescent fungi jungles of Panabuna shaped the Panasians into true survivors. They are bipedal reptilians which sport 4 muscular arms attached to impressive spines. Their 4 eyes grant them greater sight and an ability to see in the dark. Panasians were always nocturnal in nature, preferring to hunt larger mammals that would graze on the native fungi which, while common on the surface, were abundant in the gigantic caverns that were below the earth. The same caverns where future Panasian cities would grow and thrive.
